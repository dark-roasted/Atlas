# ATLAS NETWORK | OFFICIAL DOCUMENTATION

This repository contains the official website source code and community documentation for the **Atlas Network**, which includes two primary pillars: **Atlas Lounge** and **Atlas**.

---

## 🌐 Overview & Philosophy

The Atlas Network is a dual-structured ecosystem designed for modern thinkers, developers, and debaters.

### 1. Atlas Lounge
* **Focus:** Tech, Anime, Gaming, Linux ricing, and casual social interaction.
* **Atmosphere:** Chill, design-focused, and respectful.
* **Audience:** Developers, designers, and strategic thinkers.
* **Discord:** [discord.gg/atlslounge](https://discord.gg/atlslounge)

### 2. ATLAS (The Debate Server)
* **Focus:** Philosophy, Ethics, Theology, Religion, Culture, and Intellectual growth.
* **Languages:** Bilingual (Arabic & English).
* **Atmosphere:** Disciplined, rational, structured, and serious.
* **Discord:** [discord.gg/Xb6VnAbd4w](https://discord.gg/Xb6VnAbd4w)

## 📜 ATLAS SERVER RULES

1. Respect is Fundamental | No swearing, insults, personal attacks, or mocking members. Criticize the idea, not the person.
   - Any racial, religious, sexual, or ideological belittlement is strictly prohibited.
   - Penalties depend on the severity of the offense.

2. Freedom of Expression ≠ Abuse | You may discuss religions, ideas, and beliefs rationally and respectfully.
   - Strictly prohibited: Deliberate provocation, Hurtful sarcasm, Hostile or inciting speech.

3. Channel Discipline | Post each topic in its designated channel.
   - Deliberate disruption (Spam – Flood – Copy/Paste) is prohibited.
   - Sensitive topics must be discussed calmly and respectfully.

4. Follow Admin Instructions | Moderator decisions are final within the server.
   - Objections must be made respectfully via DMs or the dedicated channel.
   - Any insult or abusive language directed at any admin or staff member is grounds for a ban.

5. Severe Violations | Direct permanent ban without warnings for:
   - Severe swearing, Threats, Incitement, Spreading hate, Privacy violations, Insulting moderators/owner, or any pedophilic behavior.

PENALTY SYSTEM:
- 1st Offense: Verbal warning + explanation.
- 2nd Offense: Official written warning.
- 3rd Offense: Mute (12 to 24 hours).
- 4th Offense: Temporary ban (3 days) up to Permanent.
